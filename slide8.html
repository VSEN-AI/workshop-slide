Frontend: The Face of the App

If you can see it and click it, it is Frontend.

  • HTML
    The skeleton. It gives structure (buttons, images, text).
  • CSS / Tailwind
    The makeup. It adds beautiful colors and styling.
  • JavaScript & React
    The muscle. It makes buttons click and things move without reloading.
