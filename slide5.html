What is a Software Engineer?

A Software Engineer is a person who uses code as a tool to solve real-world problems and make human life easier.
They Build Websites
Like Amazon, Flipkart, or Netflix.
They Build Apps
Like WhatsApp, Instagram, or Zomato.
They Train AI
Like ChatGPT and Face Unlock systems.
They Keep Data Safe
Banking apps and secure passwords.
